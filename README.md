# Sales Analysis for Coffee Shop

![](https://github.com/Cuteod/Sales-Analysis-for-Coffee-Shop/blob/main/coffee.jpg)

## Introduction

In today’s competitive café market, understanding customer behaviours and sales patterns is critical for strategic growth. This analysis explores sales data from a local coffee shop covering February and March, with the aim of uncovering key insights into when, what, and how customers buy. By examining transactional data, we can help decision-makers optimize promotions, staffing, inventory, and revenue strategies.

It focuses on revenue performance by day, hour, month, payment type, and product mix. Leveraging this information can directly inform business decisions to improve profitability and customer satisfaction.

The goal is to uncover:
- When customers spend the most
- Which products drive revenue
- How payment preferences vary
- Daily, hourly, and monthly sales trends

These insights can help optimize **staffing**, **inventory**, **promotions**, and overall **business strategy**.

## Dataset Description

The dataset consists of records with:
- `Date` and `Time` of purchase
- `Coffee` or beverage type
- `Quantity sold`
- `Revenue per transaction`
- `Payment mode` (Cash or Card)

## Tools & Techniques Used

- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, formulas)
- Descriptive analytics
- Trend analysis
- Time-based sales segmentation

## Business Recommendations

### Strategic Suggestions
- **Focus promotions on weekends** to leverage peak demand.
- **Optimize staffing** for morning, lunch, and evening rush.
- **Promote card payments** to streamline operations.
- Feature **top sellers** prominently on menus and bundles.
- Target low-revenue days with discounts or special offers.

These actions can increase revenue, customer loyalty, and operational efficiency.

## Conclusion

This sales analysis provides a comprehensive view of customer behaviour and revenue patterns at the coffee shop. By identifying high-impact days, preferred products, and peak transactional hours, the business can tailor its strategy to both increase performance and improve customer satisfaction.
